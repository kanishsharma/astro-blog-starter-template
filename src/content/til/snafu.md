---
title: "SNAFU"
description: "I always thought \"snafu\" was just a fun made up word. It is actually an acronym: Situation Normal: All Fucked Up."
pubDate: "2026-05-05"
likes: 17
---

The term originated from the US Army during World War II to describe bureaucratic inefficiency.

It is a useful little word because it captures a very specific kind of brokenness: everything is technically operating, but the operating state is already messy.
